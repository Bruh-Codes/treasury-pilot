# Arbitrum Open House London — Buildathon

## Description
London is where startups, capital, and real-world adoption meet.

Arbitrum has infrastructure trusted by institutions and used by millions.

It’s time to connect the two.

The Open House London Buildathon brings three weeks of intensive building using the same tech stack that BlackRock and Robinhood bet their reputations on.

Three weeks to build something that matters.

DeFi. Gaming. Social Applications. DePin. Something entirely new?

That’s up to you.

Build with Stylus in familiar languages, or with Solidity. Deploy on Arbitrum One or a custom Orbit chain.

Bring an existing project or start from scratch.

No compromises. No limits. Just build.

And there’s plenty to play for.

The top teams will walk away with their share of a **$30,000 prize pool**, and an opportunity for **$30k in grants** in our follow-on mentorship program.

But that’s not all.

The top three teams will each win a place at the **IRL Founder House in London in June**. Four days of intense working with Arbitrum’s technical team and successful ecosystem founders.

Real mentorship, real connections, real paths from idea to funding.

We’ve built the infrastructure. You bring the vision.

Let’s see what happens next.

## Prizes

### Overall Prize — 70,000 USDC
- **First Place:** 40,000 USDC
- **Second Place:** 20,000 USDC
- **Third Place:** 10,000 USDC

#### Overall Prize Judging Criteria
Your project must be deployed on an Arbitrum chain to qualify (e.g. Arbitrum Sepolia, Arbitrum One, Robinhood Chain, or others).

Projects are assessed on:
- Smart contract quality (best practices, logical and efficient structure, minimal vulnerabilities)
- Product-market fit (clear potential to attract and retain users)
- Innovation and creativity (original approaches that push boundaries)
- Real problem solving (addresses genuine market needs)

**Note:**
- At minimum, 1 of 3 prizes is reserved for a project building on Robinhood Chain.
- At minimum, 1 of 3 prizes is reserved for a project building on Arbitrum.

### Best Agentic Project — 15,000 USDC
- **First Place:** 7,000 USDC
- **Second Place:** 5,000 USDC
- **Third Place:** 3,000 USDC

#### Best Agentic Project Judging Criteria
Your project must be deployed on an Arbitrum chain to qualify (e.g. Arbitrum Sepolia, Arbitrum One, Robinhood Chain, or others).

Projects are assessed on:
- Smart contract quality (best practices, logical and efficient structure, minimal vulnerabilities)
- Product-market fit (clear potential to attract and retain users)
- Innovation and creativity (original approaches that push boundaries)
- Real problem solving (addresses genuine market needs)

**Note:**
- At minimum, 1 of 3 prizes is reserved for a project building on Robinhood Chain.
- At minimum, 1 of 3 prizes is reserved for a project building on Arbitrum.

### Grants — 30,000 USDC
Up to **$30k USDC** in milestone-based grants for selected projects.

We reserve the right to distribute milestone-based grants to selected projects on a case-by-case basis. You are not guaranteed to be selected, and we reserve the right to not select any projects for this.

#### Grants Judging Criteria
We reserve the right to distribute milestone-based grants to select projects on a case-by-case basis. You are not guaranteed to receive a grant, and it is at the sole discretion of the Arbitrum Foundation to choose grant recipients. We reserve the right to not select any projects for grants.
